About

December 6, 2018

Vasavi Mehta, a 19 year old, is currently undertaking a bachelor's degree (Honours Prog.) in Textile and Fashion Design from Mumbai. Being a fashion a fashion design student she believes in:

"Fashion goes hand in hand with comfort."

i.e. to look good you need to feel good and to feel good you need to be comfortable! You need to be you!

"Every accomplishment starts with a desire to begin."

Hi and welcome to my world where I am going to take you all on a fun tour of all the things fashion and lifestyle. DUring the journey we might as well talk a little bit about dealing with life (basically talking about life in a wide spectrum of randomness)

Honestly starting the blog took me a lot of time and planning which did not really end up anywhere close to what I wanted and how I wanted it to turn out but then I just realized that I can't always wait for the perfect time because sometimes you have to just dare to do it and you need to breathe.. trust.. let go.. believe and see what happens. So, without any further jabber, let's get started!
